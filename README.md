## 🧠 Mental Health Statement

This project was created as an act of mental health recovery.

Getting let go isn't just losing a job. It's losing your sense of worth, your routine, your stability, and your faith in fairness itself. This site and these videos are the result of channeling that pain into creation — not as denial, but as the most defiant form of recovery there is.

**Building is therapy.**

## What's On The Site

### Chapter One — The Story
Honest account of working at Syncreon / DP World as an engineer, delivering real value, and being shown the door. Includes a journey timeline from Before → The Day → After (every project built) → Now (GCU) → Next.

### Chapter Two — Why They Let Me Go
The personal firing connected to DP World's documented corporate track record. 6 evidence cards with sourced facts:
- 32+ federal lawsuits (CourtListener, Archive.org)
- NLRB labor violations (NLRB, Reuters)
- Wage theft (Mass. AG, NLRB)
- Union suppression (NLRB, Reuters)
- Bribery convictions (Reuters, CourtListener)
- 1 engineer discarded — me

All claims sourced from public records. Links to [LegalPath](https://github.com/Turtle-PB/legalpath) Bad Company Index.

### Chapter Three — What DP World Threw Away
6 project cards showing what one dismissed engineer built alone:
- **LegalPath** (featured) — 28-view legal case builder PWA, 12K+ lines, 33+ templates, 7-layer document analysis engine, Bad Company Index
- **AutoSeq** — enterprise automotive sequencing PWA, 16 modules, 19 OEMs, SAP integration
- **OwlAI Studio** — 100% local AI platform, $0 cloud
- **RemixMii** — Suno music hub + DJ mixer, live on GitHub Pages
- **OwlEmu** — NES emulator + original 6502 assembly ROM
- **Fully Local AI Agent** — $0/month, 20+ automated tasks

### Chapter Four — Workplace Fairness & Mental Health
Advocacy section. Legally protected speech about lived experience. Mental health-aware callout: "Build anyway."

### Chapter Five — The Next Chapter
6+ apps shipped. $0 in cloud. ∞ refusal to quit. BS in progress at GCU.
