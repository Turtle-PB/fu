# FU — What They Threw Away

A personal story site by Paul Adcock / OwlLogics.

Live at: **https://turtle-pb.github.io/fu/**

## What This Is

A professional, legally-sound personal expression page telling the story of an engineer who was let go from Syncreon / DP World — and what he built afterward. No defamation. No proprietary information. Just truth, lived experience, and proof of what was thrown away.

## What's Here

- **The Story** — Honest account of being let go, told without fabrication
- **What I Built** — Showcase of AutoSeq, OwlAI Studio, RemixMii, OwlEmu, LegalPath, and more
- **Workplace Fairness & Mental Health** — Advocacy section, legally protected speech
- **The Next Chapter** — Proof that getting discarded didn't stop anything

## Tech

Single-file HTML/CSS/JS. No build step. No backend. No dependencies. Just a browser.
